StockInsight

A data science project for systematic stock market analysis — from returns and volatility to market phases and risk management.

Project Overview

In this project, we investigate various research questions about stock prices using modern data science methods. Our goal is to identify patterns and relationships in historical price data and derive well-founded insights.

Return Analysis

How are stock returns distributed? Are there systematic differences between sectors and time periods?

Volatility

How do prices fluctuate? Can volatility clusters be identified and predicted?

Market Structure

How are stocks correlated with each other? Are there natural clusters in the market?

Market Phases

Can we systematically identify and characterize bull and bear markets?

Technical Analysis

Do technical indicators like moving averages actually provide actionable signals?

Risk Management

How can portfolio risk be quantified and effectively managed?
