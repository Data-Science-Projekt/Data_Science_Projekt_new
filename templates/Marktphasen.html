Market Phases

Identification and characterization of bull and bear markets

Research Question

Can market phases (bull and bear markets) be systematically identified and what are their characteristic features?

Phase Identification

We use various approaches to detect market phases:

  • Hidden Markov Models (HMM) for regime detection
  • Rule-based approaches (20% threshold)
  • Moving averages as trend indicators

Bull Market

  • Rising prices over an extended period
  • Lower volatility
  • High trading volume
  • Positive market sentiment

Bear Market

  • Falling prices (at least -20%)
  • Elevated volatility
  • Panic-driven trading behavior
  • Negative market sentiment

Key Finding

Bull markets last significantly longer on average than bear markets, but bear markets exhibit stronger and faster price movements.
