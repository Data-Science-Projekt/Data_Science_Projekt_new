StockInsight

A data science project for systematic stock market analysis — exploring behavior, environment, risk, and fundamentals.

Project Overview

This project investigates the dynamics of the stock market using modern data science methods. We analyze historical data from tech giants and financial institutions to uncover patterns in returns, risks, and market reactions.

CATEGORY 1

Stock Behavior

Analysis of return distributions and price ranges. How do Apple and NVIDIA compare in terms of daily volatility and volume patterns?

CATEGORY 2

Market Environment

How do stocks react to extreme market stress (VIX > 30)? Exploring the correlation dynamics between Apple and Nvidia.

CATEGORY 3

Risk Analysis

Quantifying potential losses using Value-at-Risk (VaR) models. A direct risk comparison between high-growth tech and established sectors.

CATEGORY 4

Sentiment & Macro

Does news sentiment drive Apple's volatility? We analyze the correlation between consumer sentiment index and stock returns.

CATEGORY 5

Company Fundamentals

Investigating product-to-stock interdependence. How do iPhone sales figures impact Apple's long-term stock performance?
